# Product Management Guide for Clients

## Welcome to Your Product Management System! 🎉

This simple guide will help you add and manage products on your website using Sanity Studio.

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Step 1: Access the Admin Panel

1. Open your web browser
2. Go to: `https://yourdomain.com/studio` (or `http://localhost:3000/studio` for testing)
3. Click **"Sign in"** and use your Sanity credentials

### Step 2: Navigate to Products

- After logging in, you'll see the Sanity Studio dashboard
- Click **"Product"** in the left sidebar
- You'll see a list of all your products

---

## ➕ Adding a New Product

### Step-by-Step Instructions:

1. **Click the "+ Create" button** (top right corner)

2. **Fill in Basic Information:**
   - **Product Name**: Full name (e.g., "MacBook Pro 14-inch M3")
   - **URL Slug**: Click "Generate" button (creates the web address automatically)
   - **SKU**: Your product code (e.g., "LP-MACBOOK-001")
   - **Brand**: Manufacturer name (e.g., "Apple", "Dell", "HP")
   - **Category**: Select from dropdown

3. **Upload Images:**
   - Scroll to "Product Images" section
   - Click **"Upload"** or drag & drop images
   - **First image = Main product image** (drag to reorder)
   - For each image:
     - Click the image
     - Add "Alternative Text" (describe the image - required for SEO)
     - Add optional caption
   - You can upload up to 10 images

4. **Add Descriptions:**
   - **Short Description**: 1-2 sentences for product listings
   - **Full Description**: Detailed information with formatting
     - Use the toolbar to make text **bold** or _italic_
     - Create bullet lists or numbered lists
     - Add headings if needed

5. **Set Price:**
   - **Price (BDT)**: Regular price in Taka (৳)
   - **Sale Price**: Only fill this if product is on discount

6. **Set Stock:**
   - **Stock Quantity**: How many units you have
   - **Low Stock Alert**: You'll be notified when stock drops below this number
   - **In Stock**: Toggle ON if available

7. **Product Status:**
   - ✅ **Active**: Product is visible on website _(recommended)_
   - 📝 **Draft**: Hidden from website (use while preparing)
   - 📦 **Archived**: For discontinued products

8. **Optional: Add Technical Specifications**
   - Expand "Technical Specifications" section
   - Fill in relevant details:
     - Processor
     - RAM
     - Storage
     - Display
     - Graphics Card
     - Battery Life
     - Weight
     - Operating System
     - Ports & Connectivity

9. **Optional: Add Warranty Info**
   - Warranty Period (e.g., "1 Year")
   - Warranty Type (e.g., "International")
   - Additional details

10. **Optional: Add Tags**
    - Add keywords like "gaming", "business", "ultrabook"
    - Helps customers find products

11. **Publish Your Product**
    - Click the **"Publish"** button (top right, green button)
    - Your product is now LIVE on the website! 🎉

## ✏️ Editing an Existing Product

1. Click **"Product"** in left sidebar
2. Find your product in the list (use search box if needed)
3. Click on the product to open it
4. Make your changes
5. Click **"Publish"** to save changes

**Note:** Changes appear on the website within 30-60 seconds.

---

## 🖼️ Managing Images

### Adding More Images:

1. Open the product
2. Scroll to "Product Images"
3. Click "Upload" and select new images
4. Click "Publish"

### Removing Images:

1. Click on the image you want to remove
2. Click the **trash icon** (🗑️) or press Delete key
3. Confirm deletion
4. Click "Publish"

### Reordering Images:

1. Click and hold the **drag handle** (⋮⋮) on the left of image
2. Drag to new position
3. First image = main product image
4. Click "Publish"

### Editing Image Details:

1. Click on any image
2. Update "Alternative Text" (describes image for search engines)
3. Add or edit caption (optional)
4. Click "Publish"

## 📊 Managing Stock

### Update Stock Quantity:

1. Open the product
2. Find "Stock Quantity" field
3. Enter new number
4. Click "Publish"

### Mark as Out of Stock:

1. Set "Stock Quantity" to **0**
2. Toggle "In Stock" to **OFF**
3. Click "Publish"

### Low Stock Alerts:

- Set "Low Stock Alert Threshold" (e.g., 5)
- You'll be notified when stock drops below this number

---

## 💰 Managing Prices

### Regular Price:

- Enter price in **Price (BDT)** field
- Use numbers only (no ৳ symbol needed)

### Sale/Discount Price:

- Enter discounted price in **Sale Price (BDT)**
- Leave empty to remove discount
- Sale price will show on website if lower than regular price

## 🔍 Best Practices

### For Images:

✅ **DO:**

- Use high-quality images (at least 800x800 pixels)
- Use square images (1:1 ratio) for consistency
- Use white or transparent backgrounds
- Upload 3-5 images per product (different angles)
- Add descriptive "Alternative Text" to every image

❌ **DON'T:**

- Use blurry or low-quality images
- Upload huge files (keep under 5MB)
- Forget to add alternative text

### For Descriptions:

✅ **DO:**

- Write clear, customer-focused descriptions
- Highlight key features and benefits
- Use bullet points for specifications
- Check spelling and grammar

❌ **DON'T:**

- Copy-paste from manufacturers (write unique descriptions)
- Use ALL CAPS or excessive punctuation!!!
- Leave descriptions empty

### For Product Management:

✅ **DO:**

- Keep stock quantities accurate
- Use consistent SKU format
- Update prices regularly
- Archive discontinued products
- Use descriptive product names

❌ **DON'T:**

- Delete products (use Archive instead)
- Duplicate products unnecessarily
- Leave status as "Draft" for live products

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### Problem: Can't upload images

**Solutions:**

- Check image file size (must be under 5MB)
- Try a different image format (JPG or PNG)
- Try a different browser (Chrome recommended)
- Clear browser cache and try again

### Problem: Changes not showing on website

**Solutions:**

- Wait 30-60 seconds after publishing
- Hard refresh: Press `Ctrl + Shift + R` (Windows) or `Cmd + Shift + R` (Mac)
- Make sure product status is "Active"
- Check that you clicked "Publish" (not just save)

### Problem: Can't access Studio

**Solutions:**

- Check your internet connection
- Verify you're using the correct URL
- Make sure you're logged in
- Contact your website administrator

### Problem: Product not appearing on website

**Solutions:**

- Make sure "Product Status" is set to "Active"
- Check that "In Stock" is toggled ON
- Verify that at least one image is uploaded
- Make sure you clicked "Publish"
